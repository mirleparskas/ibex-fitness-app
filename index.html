How The Plan Progresses

Weeks 1-3 build volume and load. Week 4 tests the first block. Weeks 5-7 intensify with heavier pulls and sharper metcons. Week 8 retests.

Editable By Design

The program content is in workouts.js. Change text there and refresh the browser. The layout and saved progress keep working.

Use current 1RMs for percentages. Pick the conditioning tier that preserves the intended stimulus. Checked workouts, selected tiers, notes, and scores sync through this app server when opened from phone or computer.

This is a workout display tool, not medical advice. Scale loads and volume to current ability, recovery, and technique.
