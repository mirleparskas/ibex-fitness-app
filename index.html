How The Plan Progresses

Weeks 1-3 rebuild volume and skill. Week 4 changes the stimulus and lowers stress. Weeks 5-7 push heavier glute work, cleans, push press, and bodybuilding volume. Week 8 retests clean reps and conditioning.

Weekly Structure

Each day has a different job: thrust, clean/gymnastics, squat, upper bodybuilding/push press, hinge, mixed modal, and recovery. Cardio stays structured instead of rotating randomly.

Use RPE, clean reps, progressive overload, and repeatable conditioning. Keep protein high, eat enough to recover, and use cardio as conditioning rather than punishment. Checked workouts, notes, and scores sync here.
