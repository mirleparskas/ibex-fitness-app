How The Plan Progresses

Weeks 1-3 rebuild volume and clean reps. Week 4 changes the stimulus and lowers load. Weeks 5-7 build load, reps, and density. Week 8 retests repeatable conditioning, Olympic technique, and controlled strength.

Weekly Structure

Default layout: Monday lower/glutes, Tuesday upper push, Wednesday recovery cardio, Thursday Olympic technique and upper pull, Friday posterior/glutes, Saturday optional full-body pump, and Sunday recovery cardio.

On EMOM, AMRAP, and chipper days, lift moderate with good form only. On Zone 2 days, lift more normally. Keep protein high, eat enough to recover, and use cardio as conditioning rather than punishment.

This is a workout display tool, not medical advice. Scale loads and volume to current ability, recovery, and technique.
