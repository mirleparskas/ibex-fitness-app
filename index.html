How The Plan Progresses

Weeks 1-2 establish baselines at roughly 3 RIR. Weeks 3-4 load at roughly 2 RIR. Weeks 5-7 progress productive work at 1-2 RIR. Week 8 deloads sets, sprint volume, jumps, and conditioning while preserving movement skill.

Weekly Structure

The seven sessions begin in a sensible recovery order, but they are not weekdays. Move them freely. Weekly objectives and recovery warnings show what each change preserves, removes, or overloads.

Settings

On EMOM, AMRAP, and chipper days, lift moderate with good form only. On Zone 2 days, lift more normally. Keep protein high, eat enough to recover, and use cardio as conditioning rather than punishment.

This is a workout display tool, not medical advice. Scale loads and volume to current ability, recovery, and technique.
