Muscle + Conditioning Split

A restart-friendly plan built around 30-45 minutes of cardio, HIIT, EMOMs, StairMaster, or intervals followed by 30-40 minutes of lifting. Three days bias glutes and three days train full body for muscle, shape, and tone.

How The Plan Progresses

Weeks 1-3 restart with moderate loads and repeatable effort. Week 4 backs off slightly. Weeks 5-7 build heavier or denser sets. Week 8 gives you a smooth rep-max or best-effort benchmark without needing Olympic lifts.

Editable By Design

Use RPE and clean reps while rebuilding. Cardio can be running, StairMaster, HIIT, EMOMs, incline walking, or machines. Checked workouts, notes, and scores sync through this app server when opened from phone or computer.
